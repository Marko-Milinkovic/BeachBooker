About this beach bar

Blue Horizon Beach Club

+3 photos

Blue Horizon Beach Club sits on a 120-metre stretch of fine pebble beach along Budva’s southern coastline, backed by Mediterranean pines that give natural shade to the rear rows. The water is crystal-clear with a gentle slope — perfect for swimming and safe for small children wading near the shore.

The beach is divided into four zones. Premium front-row spots are right at the waterline with dedicated umbrella service. Standard spots sit one row back with full sun all day — ideal for tanning. Lazy Bags are comfy bean-bag loungers perfect for a relaxed afternoon, while private Cabanas at the back offer shade, curtains, and a touch of luxury for couples or small groups.

On-site you’ll find a fully stocked beach bar serving cocktails, cold beer, and fresh juices, plus a grill restaurant with seafood, salads, and light Mediterranean bites — all delivered straight to your spot if you prefer. Free showers, changing cabins, and clean restrooms are available near the entrance.

Parking is just a two-minute walk away (reserved spots available as an add-on). Free Wi-Fi covers the entire beach. The vibe is relaxed and family-friendly during the day, with occasional live acoustic sets on weekend evenings — no loud beach parties, just good music and sunset views. The bar stays open until 20:00.
